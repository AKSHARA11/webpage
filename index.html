Laboratory for Computational Social Systems (LCS2) is a research group led by Dr. Tanmoy Chakraborty at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi (IIIT-Delhi). Broad research interests of this group includes Data Mining, Complex Networks, Social Computing, Natural Language Processing and Data-driven Cybersecurity.

Current Openings

  • There is one PhD position and one RA position available under a DST-funded project. The work will involve data mining, data-driven understanding of different security aspects in online media. Interested candidates may directly send their CV to Dr. Tanmoy Chakraborty (tanmoy@iiitd.ac.in) or lcs2@iiitd.ac.in with the subject line "Interested for PhD position under DST project".

For internship opportunities, please send your CV/Resume to lcs2@iiitd.ac.in
